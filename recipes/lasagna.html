Homemade Lasagna

What to Expect

Savor the warmth of a homemade lasagna, where layers of fresh pasta, savory bolognese sauce and seasoned ground beef unite in perfect harmony. Topped with gooey melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, each bite is a blissful symphony of flavor and texture, delivering comfort and satisfaction with every forkful. Experience the essence of Italian tradition and culinary delight in every delicious slice.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Onion
  • 5 Cloves of Garlic
  • 700g of Ground Beef(we recommend topside)
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh lasagna sheets
  • Fresh mozzarella

How To

    Bolognese Sauce

  1. First fry the onions and chopped galic in a table spoon of olive oil, and when the onions start look translucent they are ready
  2. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook it util it has loosen up
  3. Now add the tomato sauce to the pan and cook for 8 minutes

    Layering Our Lasagna

  1. Make a nice layer of your Bolognese sauce at the bottom of the casserole
  2. Add fresh lasagna sheets until it fully covers the suace
  3. Add the fresh mozzarella on top of the sauce
  4. Repeat the porcess util the casserole is full
  5. At the top, add a generous amount of mozzarella and grated permeggiano cheese
